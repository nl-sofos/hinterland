1.3 Hinterland Forward!
  • The government is going to set-up innovation centers in every village / neighborhood
  • Places for children and adults, man and women alike.
  • From there they can learn and innovate
  • With electricity, computers and appropriate tools
  • And of course a mast to give radio, tv, cell phone and internet connection
  • These all are enablers for a range of new developments.
  • They will help us realize the UN Sustainable Development Goals
