2.3 Community building
The people can use cell phones to call each other, especially their family in other villages and in the city
By the range of telecommunications means, each village can cooperate more efficiently with other villages.
In the center people can help each other to find answers to whatever questions. They also can be helped by specialists elsewhere.
The Chrystallization center will also be the place were the people meet the government: registration of births, marriages etc.
The center is the base for collecting statistical data
The center will run a local weather station
