2.2 Our main problem is that nobody knows Hinterland.
2.2.1 Hinterland is about 400 x 400 km
2.2.2 90% of the area is covered by tropical rain forest
2.2.3 We have wood, fruit, nuts, minerals, many rivers and plenty of sunshine. We can feed ourselves but we have barely any money for other goods and services.
2.2.4 We have only one city, the capital
2.2.5 We are 1 million people in total
2.2.6 And nobody seems to know us
