2.3 Even in our capital they forget the hinterland
2.3.1 The capital city and its surroundings have electricity, telecom, companies, a few hospitals and schools for all children
2.3.2 Government has been focussed on the capital city; If the capital city flourishes, the hinterland will improve automatically, they think
2.3.3 They are wrong; no teacher is willing to work here; we are too isolated
2.3.4 The only and state owned electricity company does not enter the hinterland. Although they promidded to study the potential of local hydropower (some years ago).
2.3.5 The three telecom providers committed to connect the hinterland, but they are still hesitating to do so. One of them is still state owned.The other two complain about false competition.
