2.2 SDG 3: Ensure healthy lives and promote wellbeing for all at all ages
  • The Innovation Centers contribute to the improvement of health by bringing ict and internet to the local health workers
  • The local nurse can keep better records for her patients
  • She can run more measurements and tests to make the weekly doctor's visit more efficient
  • She will have better resources to consult doctors far away
  • She can download urgent information on diseases like Zika and distribute it immediately
  • She can organize special meetings with multimedia on early childcare, drugs, etc.
  • Social media (as well as radio and TV) become available to send health information to the population
