1.1 Countrywide discussion process
 
  • Last year government organized a large bottom-up consultation process
  • Dedicated teams visited every village and neighborhood; more than 200 in total
  • They collected data about the actual condition
  • They organized numerous meetings with the people
  • These meetings resulted in long lists of issues 
  • The teams have processed them and came up with priorities
  • The "Hinterland Forward!" program is the result
